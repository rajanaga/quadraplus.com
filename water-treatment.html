Stainless Steel / FRP Filter Body

Our media filters come in three durable materials: FRP, carbon steel, and stainless steel. FRP is corrosion-resistant and lightweight, ideal for high humidity or aggressive conditions. Carbon steel offers robust construction for high- pressure applications, perfect for large commercial installations. Stainless steel provides exceptional corrosion resistance and lasting performance for both residential and commercial use.

Ultra Membranes (Gray Water Recycling)

Gray water is wastewater from sinks, showers, and laundry, excluding sewage. It's often recycled for non-potable uses like:

  • Residential: Toilet flushing, garden irrigation, laundry, car washing.
  • Commercial: Toilet flushing, landscape irrigation, cooling towers, cleaning.
  • Industrial: Process water, machinery cooling, dust control, green spaces.
  • Agricultural: Crop irrigation, livestock watering, greenhouses.
  • Public Spaces: Park irrigation, sports fields, golf courses, firefighting reserves.
